<p align="justify">
Tudo sobre sistema de banco de dados: dos fundamentos aos novos conceitos. Com questões práticas e teóricas, este livro apresenta aplicação, gerenciamento e implementação de banco de dados e inclui os assuntos recentes da área, como Big Data e blockchain. Como diferencial, Sistema de Banco de Dados apresenta conceitos e algoritmos em um ambiente geral, não ligados a um sistema de dados específico, o que flexibiliza a aplicação da teoria a cenários diversos. </p>

Com conteúdo atualizado, esta obra é ideal para estudantes de análise de redes, análise de sistemas processamento de dados e engenharia da computação que queiram dominar o assunto e destacar-se na área.

### Description

<p align="justify">

With practical and theoretical questions, this book presents the application, management, and implementation of databases and includes recent topics in the field, such as Big Data and blockchain. As a differentiator, Database System presents concepts and algorithms in a general environment, not tied to a specific data system, which makes it flexible to apply the theory to diverse scenarios. In addition, it offers a variety of exercises at different levels of complexity and learning-support tools.This edition of Database System is necessary because it brings:Extensive coverage of Big Data systems, from the user's point of view, as well as an internal perspective;Updates (in every chapter) on the internal aspects of databases, to reflect state-of-the-art technology, such as solid-state drives (SSD), main-memory databases, multi-core systems, and parallel and distributed databases;Advanced coverage of semi-structured data management using JSON, RDF, and SPARQL;Updated coverage of temporal data, data analysis, and advanced indexing techniques, such as write-optimized indexes;Reorganization and updating of the chapters to better support courses, with a significant practical component, including the use of state-of-the-art tools for application development and Big Data systems, such as Apache Hadoop and Spark;A new chapter on Blockchain Databases, which introduces the technology and its increasingly important role in enterprise applications.
</p>

<p align="justify">

In this edition, new chapters were introduced on recent advances in database systems and Big Data processing, an introduction to NOSQL databases, and technologies for big data processing. In addition, new examples were included, and the content on query processing and optimization and its techniques, strategies, and algorithms for processing was expanded, and new SQL constructs were added. With updated content, this work is ideal for students of network analysis, systems analysis, data processing, and computer engineering who want to master the subject and stand out in the field.
