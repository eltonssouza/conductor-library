### Description

Guidorizzi, the complete content for your edge! By Hamilton Luiz Guidorizzi, A Calculus Course offers digital pedagogical resources that deepen and reinforce the learning of Calculus students and assist teachers in the subject. This third volume covers topics such as double integrals, triple integrals, line integrals, among others. It is recommended for undergraduate students of Mathematics, Engineering, and Exact Sciences who take Differential and Integral Calculus and related Calculus subjects.
