### Description

In this 9th edition, as in all previous ones, the authors maintain the tradition of writing a book that helps students discover calculus – both for its practical usefulness and for its surprising beauty. The aim is to convey to the reader a sense of the usefulness of calculus, as well as to promote the development of their technical skill. At the same time, the authors strove to highlight the intrinsic beauty of the subject. There is no doubt that Newton experienced a sense of triumph when he made his great discoveries. The goal of this work is to have students share a little of that enthusiasm.

### Description

Guidorizzi, the complete content for your edge! By Hamilton Luiz Guidorizzi, A Calculus Course offers digital pedagogical resources that deepen and reinforce the learning of Calculus students and assist teachers in the subject. This second volume covers topics such as limits, derivatives, integrals of functions, among others. It is recommended for undergraduate students of Mathematics, Engineering, and Exact Sciences who take Differential and Integral Calculus and related Calculus subjects.
