## Software Engineering 

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Authors | SOMMERVILLE, I. |
| Publication Year | 2019 |
| Edition | 10th edition. |
| ISBN | 9788543024974 |

### Description

The 10th edition of Software Engineering has been extensively updated to reflect the growing adoption of agile methods in software engineering. One of the highlights of the new edition is the addition of content on the Scrum methodology. The book's four-part structure has been significantly reworked to accommodate new chapters on resilience engineering, systems engineering, and systems of systems. In addition, chapters on topics such as dependability, security, and safety have been completely reorganized. All of these changes are justified by addressing essential issues for modern software engineering ― managing complexity, integrating agility with other methods, and ensuring that systems are secure and resilient. A reference work for students of computer science, computer engineering, and information systems, and for any professional who wishes to update their knowledge on software reuse, project architecture, reliability and security, and systems engineering.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Authors | PRESSMAN, Roger S.. |
| Publication Year | 2021 |
| Edition | 9th edition. |
| ISBN | 9786558040101 |

### Description

The new edition of the market-leading textbook in software engineering brings a series of new features. The book has been fully revised and restructured to improve its pedagogical flow. It introduces new and important processes and practices in the field, among them the use of artificial intelligence and machine learning to ensure speed and quality in deliveries. The author emphasizes aspects of software quality, especially in the use of agile methods. Security is another item that has gained prominence.

## Software Engineering - Products - Vol.1 and 2

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Authors | PÁDUA FILHO, W. |
| Publication Year | 2019 |
| Edition | 4th edition. |
| ISBN | 9788521636687 e 9788521636694 |

### Description

A reference for Computing and related courses. By Wilson de Pádua Paula Filho, the book Software Engineering – Products is a tool for students and professionals as it presents relevant content on the maintenance and development of software. Now in two volumes, this first part is related to the Products area and provides an introduction to the subject, maintaining a more technical profile. It concentrates on foundational chapters, presenting topics such as basic UML, requirements, analysis, design, testing, and implementation, in addition to the most relevant and detailed standards of the technical methods of the "classic" Praxis process (SPraxis) and its respective "agile" version (XPraxis). The treatment is based on object-oriented technology. Its analysis and design notation is UML 2.0, and the standards follow those established by the Software Engineering of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), keeping up with all the updates made. With supplementary material for teachers, Software Engineering covers topics required by various undergraduate courses, such as Informatics, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Systems Analysis, Data Processing, among others. Why read Software Engineering? Computerized systems have enormous potential to bring benefits, as well as harm when designed incorrectly. Since software is the soul of computerized systems, Software Engineering is the discipline that teaches how to build real products from the fundamental concepts of Informatics. Thus, this book presents the most established practices of this discipline.
