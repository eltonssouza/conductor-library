Expanded and completely revised by the authors, the book features hundreds of new examples and exercises, as well as fully redrawn illustrations. It has a new layout that makes for more pleasant reading and facilitates the comprehension and location of topics and exercises, while the successful didactic structure of previous editions has been carefully preserved.
