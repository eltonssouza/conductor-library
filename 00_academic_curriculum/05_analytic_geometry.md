## Analytic Geometry: A Vector Treatment

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Authors | BOULOS, P., CAMARGO, I. |
| Publication Year | 2004 |
| Edition | 3rd edition. |
| ISBN | 9788587918918 |

### Description

The new edition of Analytic Geometry - A Vector Treatment confirms its position as a classic of the exact sciences. Expanded and completely revised by the authors, the book features hundreds of new examples and exercises, as well as fully redrawn illustrations. It has a new layout that makes for more pleasant reading and facilitates the comprehension and location of topics and exercises, while the successful didactic structure of previous editions has been carefully preserved. Written in clear and objective language, this book provides answers to the exercises and solution strategies, making it an essential guide for the study of Geometry.
