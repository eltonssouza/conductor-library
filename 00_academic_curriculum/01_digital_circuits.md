O Capítulo 1 foi extensivamente revisado e atualizado e novos problemas e exemplos foram adicionados em todo o livro. Sistemas digitais é leitura fundamental para cursos de engenharia, assim como para disciplinas ministradas nos cursos de automação, ciência da computação e eletrônica, entre outros.

### Description

<p align="justify">
This book provides a comprehensive study of the principles and techniques of modern digital systems. In addition to teaching the fundamentals of the subject, it covers in detail both modern and traditional methods of applying digital design and development techniques, including how to manage a system-level project. In this 12th edition, obsolete technologies have been replaced or abridged to offer only content appropriate to modern systems. Chapter 1 has been extensively revised and updated, and new problems and examples have been added throughout the book. Digital Systems is essential reading for engineering courses, as well as for subjects taught in automation, computer science, and electronics courses, among others.
</p>
