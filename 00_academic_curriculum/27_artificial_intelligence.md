## Artificial Intelligence - A Machine Learning Approach

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Authors | André Carlos Ponce de Leon Ferreira et al. |
| Publication Year | 2021 |
| Edition | 2nd edition. |
| ISBN | 9788521637349 |

### Description

Artificial Intelligence under new perspectives. With a balanced approach to basic and advanced topics developed by renowned researchers in the field, this new edition brings examples of implementations of the methods covered as well as theoretical and practical exercises. Winner of the 2012 Jabuti Award (Technology and Computing Category) when it was released, Artificial Intelligence – A Machine Learning Approach covers the main concepts and algorithms of machine learning and points out ways to transform this knowledge into practical applications, such as: aiding the diagnosis of diseases and the discovery of more effective treatments; monitoring water quality; preventing forest fires; detecting and locating faults in power transmission lines; image recognition to provide security at events; blocking fraudulent messages that pose risks to users, among others. Why have Artificial Intelligence? For some time now, the field of artificial intelligence has ceased to be seen as merely theoretical – intended for application to small "curious" problems – to become a growing field of research, seeking solutions to real problems in society. Katti Faceli, Ana Carolina Lorena, João Gama, Tiago Agostinho Almeida, and André Carlos Ponce Carvalho successfully fill the gap of comprehensive and up-to-date books aimed at the national audience.

### Description

With a clear and objective approach, George F. Luger brings together in this sixth edition the most recent practices in the market and the important topics related to artificial intelligence, such as emergent computation, representation of uncertainty, and probabilistic natural language processing, among others. Very rich in practical examples, this work is ideal for students of computer science, computer engineering, and information systems, as it presents a balance between theory and practice and the fundamental concepts of the field, with the details and information necessary for implementing the algorithms and strategies discussed in the book.

## Artificial Intelligence

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Authors | COPPIN, B. |
| Publication Year | 2010 |
| Edition | 1st edition. |
| ISBN | 9788521617297 |

### Description

The book covers a variety of Artificial Intelligence techniques, algorithms, and methodologies, including game playing, intelligent agents, machine learning, genetic algorithms, and artificial life. With straightforward and accessible text, the author focuses on explaining how Artificial Intelligence techniques relate to and are derived from natural systems, such as the human brain and the evolution of species itself, explaining how the artificial equivalents are used in the real world. Each chapter includes an introduction explaining what will be covered, a chapter summary, some exercises and review questions, as well as suggestions for further reading. There is a complete bibliography at the end of the book, in addition to a glossary including brief definitions of most of the important terms.
